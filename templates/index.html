COMPANY INTRODUCTION

Who We are?

Networld group is a leading ICT solutions provider in Bangladesh, providing different IT products and services to various banks and financial institutions across the country. Our company is certified under ISO 9001:2015. Our range of services also includes logistics, courier, physical security, managed services, software, and maintenance services through our dedicated team of certified engineers. We also represent many industry-leading partners like NCR, DPTF, Hikvision, Huawei, Certis Cisco, Urovo, Software AG, BPC, Kimaya, Glory, ARCA among many others. We are also involved in sports development as part of our corporate social responsibility and are one of the co-organizers of the Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Dhaka Marathon working with the Bangladesh Army as the organizer.
